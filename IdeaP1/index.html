About Keshi

Casey Luong better known as Keshi is a Viernamese-American singer, songwriter, and producer.
He gained recognition on SoundCloud and Youtube with his music that often blends elements
of R&B, pop and alternative, characterized by its emotional depth and mellow, melodic style.

Requiem

Requiem is the stunning new album from Keshi. Made in Houston, LA,
New York and Tokyo, Requiem will bring 13 songs that shows Keshi's
incredible skills as a writer, producer and performer. In this moment Keshi
released two single of this album Say and Dream giving us a glipse of what is coming.
